Track Side Ops — Desktop

A native window for the Track Side Ops web app. A couple things to know before you start:

  • ↻
    Always up to date This app loads tracksideops.com directly, so every new feature and fix appears the next time you open it — no reinstalling.
  • ↗
    External links open in your browser Anything that points outside tracksideops.com (help articles, social links, payment receipts) opens in your default web browser, not inside this window.
  • ⌘
    Native shortcuts work Use the View menu to reload, zoom, or go full screen. Standard copy / paste / undo all work as expected.
